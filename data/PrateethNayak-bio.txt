cross-disciplinary machine learning & deep learning. He received a Masters
in Electrical & Comp. Engineering from the University of Minnesota, Twin Cities
in 2019, and he received a Bachelors in Electronics & Computer Science from the Visvesvaraya
Technological University in 2017. He was also part of Prof. Mingyi Hong group at UMN
contributing to multiple projects during his Masters.
